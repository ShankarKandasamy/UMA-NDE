# 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

This document establishes the site-specific requirements for Non-Destructive Examination (NDE) activities performed at the Great Lakes Chemical Processing Hamilton Plant. These requirements supplement and, where specified, supersede the requirements of referenced codes and standards. All NDE contractors performing work at this facility shall comply with the requirements herein.
1.1 Applicability
This specification applies to all NDE activities including but not limited to: ultrasonic thickness measurement (UT), magnetic particle testing (MT), liquid penetrant testing (PT), visual inspection (VT), and radiographic testing (RT) performed on pressure-containing equipment and piping systems.
