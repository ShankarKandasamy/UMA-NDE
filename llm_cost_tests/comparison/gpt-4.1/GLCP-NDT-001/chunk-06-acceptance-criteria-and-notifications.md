# Acceptance Criteria and Notifications

Minimum Thickness: Calculated per ASME B31.3 or original code. t-min per circuit is provided in inspection data.
Alert Thresholds: 
- CRITICAL: Reading < t-min — stop, immediate notification.
- Alert: Reading < 110% t-min — notify within same shift.
- Monitor: Corrosion rate > 10 mpy — document and recommend.
- Acceptable: All other readings — document only.
Notification (CRITICAL): Cease work, verify, contact GLCP Coordinator, remain until arrival, photograph/findings, confirm with rep.
