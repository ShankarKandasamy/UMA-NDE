# 6. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA AND NOTIFICATIONS

6.1 Minimum Thickness (t-min)
Minimum required thickness shall be calculated in accordance with ASME B31.3 or the original design code. t-min values for each piping circuit are provided in the inspection data packages. Readings below t-min require immediate notification per Section 6.3.
6.2 Alert Thresholds
Condition Threshold Required Action
CRITICAL Reading < t-min STOP - Immediate notification to GLCP
Alert Reading < 110% of t-min Notify GLCP within same shift
Monitor Corrosion rate > 10 mpy Document in report, recommend action
Acceptable All other readings Document in report
6.3 Notification Procedures
For CRITICAL findings (readings below t-min), the contractor shall: 1. Immediately cease work at the affected location 2. Verify the reading with repeat measurement 3. Contact GLCP Inspection Coordinator by radio or phone 4. Do not leave the area until GLCP representative arrives 5. Document the finding with photographs and detailed notes 6. Confirm reading with GLCP representative present
