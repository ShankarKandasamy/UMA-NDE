# 6.3 Notification Procedures

For CRITICAL findings (readings below t-min), the contractor shall: 1. Immediately cease work at the affected location. 2. Verify the reading with repeat measurement. 3. Contact GLCP Inspection Coordinator by radio or phone. 4. Do not leave the area until GLCP representative arrives. 5. Document the finding with photographs and detailed notes. 6. Confirm reading with GLCP representative present. GLCP Inspection Coordinator: Jennifer Walsh, (905) 555-0147, Radio Channel 3.
