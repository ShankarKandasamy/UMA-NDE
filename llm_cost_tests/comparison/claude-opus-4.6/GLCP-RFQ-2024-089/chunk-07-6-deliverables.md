# 6. DELIVERABLES

The Contractor shall provide the following deliverables:
• Draft Inspection Report - Within 5 business days of field work completion
• Final Inspection Report (PDF) - Within 2 business days of GLCP comments
• Raw Data Files (CSV/Excel) - With draft report
• Photographs (organized by CML) - With draft report
• Calibration Certificates - Prior to mobilization
• Personnel Certifications - Prior to mobilization
• Daily Field Reports - End of each work day
