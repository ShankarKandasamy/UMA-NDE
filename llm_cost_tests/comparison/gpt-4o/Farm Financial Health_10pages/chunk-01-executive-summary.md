# Executive Summary

Taking Stock of Farm Financial Health Amid Rising Costs of Production and a Rapidly Changing Climate. Canadian farms face increased challenges due to external domestic and global pressures like COVID-19, climate change, and the Ukraine war. In 2022, primary agriculture contributed $36.3 billion to Canada's GDP and supported 249,000 jobs. Despite manageable debt levels, rising production costs and climate impacts press serious concerns for mental health in rural areas.
