# 4. EQUIPMENT REQUIREMENTS

4.1 Ultrasonic Thickness Gauges: All UT thickness gauges shall meet the following minimum requirements: Digital display with minimum resolution of 0.001 inch, Data logging capability with minimum 1000 reading storage, Capable of operating in temperature range 32°F to 120°F, Battery life sufficient for full shift operation (minimum 8 hours), Dual or multi-element transducer capability for high-temperature applications.
